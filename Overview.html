Overview

A new invisible enemy, only 30kb in size, has emerged from Wuhan, China and is on a killing spree around the world: 2019-nCoV, the Novel Coronavirus! The first case in Nepal was confirmed on 23 January 2020 when a 31-year-old student, who had returned to Kathmandu from Wuhan on 9 January, tested positive for the disease.
